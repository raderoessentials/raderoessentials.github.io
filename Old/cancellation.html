Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Radero Essentials, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Cancellation & Refund Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for cancelling an order and requesting a refund. By placing an order with us, you agree to adhere to the following terms:

Order Cancellation: You may cancel your order within 3 days of placing it, as long as it has not been processed or shipped. To cancel an order, please contact our customer support team immediately with your order details. We will do our best to accommodate your cancellation request.

Refund Eligibility: Refunds may be provided under the following circumstances:

Order Cancellation: If you cancel your order within the specified timeframe mentioned above and it meets the cancellation criteria, you will be eligible for a full refund.

Defective or Damaged Products: If you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact our customer support team within 3 days of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or offer a refund after assessing the issue.

Return Process: To initiate a return or refund, please follow these steps: Contact our customer support team with your order details and reason for the return/refund request. Our team will provide you with further instructions on returning the product. Please ensure that the product is returned in its original condition, unused, with all tags and packaging intact.

Refund Processing: Once your returned product is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method within [X business days].

Non-Refundable Items: Please note that certain items are non-refundable, including:

Products that have been personalized or customized. Products that have been used, washed, or damaged due to misuse. Clearance or sale items.

Shipping Costs: Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective/damaged product. In such cases, we will provide a prepaid shipping label or reimburse your return shipping costs.

Contact Us: If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding cancellations, refunds, or returns, please contact our customer support team at raderoessentials@gmail.com. We are here to help you.
